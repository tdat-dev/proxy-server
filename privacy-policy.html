Privacy Policy for QuickTranslate AI

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

Overview

QuickTranslate AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension DOES NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. We respect your privacy and only process the text you actively choose to translate.

Data We Process (Temporarily)

  • Selected Text: When you select text and click the translate button, that text is sent to our server and Google Gemini AI for translation. This text is processed immediately and NOT stored on our servers.
  • Translation Cache: Translations are cached locally on your device only using Chrome's storage API for 72 hours to improve performance. This cache never leaves your device.
  • Language Preferences: Your preferred target language is saved locally on your device only for convenience.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, age, phone number, etc.)
  • ❌ Browsing history or list of websites you visit
  • ❌ User activity tracking (clicks, mouse movements, keystrokes)
  • ❌ Location data or IP addresses
  • ❌ Authentication credentials (passwords, login information)
  • ❌ Financial information
  • ❌ Health information
  • ❌ Website content (we only process text you explicitly select, not store it)

Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All settings and cached translations are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
  • No Server Storage: Our server does not store any translations or user data after processing.
  • Automatic Deletion: Cached translations are automatically deleted after 72 hours.

Third-Party Services

Translation requests are processed through Google Gemini AI. When you translate text:

  • The selected text is sent to Google's API for translation
  • Google processes the text and returns the translation
  • The text is immediately discarded after processing
  • We do not send any identifying information to Google

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how Google handles data.

Data Sharing

  • ❌ We do NOT sell data to third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT share data with advertisers
  • ❌ We do NOT use data for purposes unrelated to translation
  • ❌ We do NOT use data for creditworthiness or lending purposes

Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To read text you select and display translation results on the current page only when you interact with the extension.
  • storage: To save your language preferences and cache translations locally on your device.
  • scripting: To inject the translation button and popup interface into webpages.
  • contextMenus: To add a "Translate" option to the right-click menu.
  • Host Permission (https://proxy-server-4lb3.onrender.com/*): To send translation requests to our API server.

User Control

  • Opt-in Only: Translation only happens when you actively select text and click the translate button.
  • Clear Data: You can clear cached translations anytime by clearing Chrome extension data.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the extension removes all local data immediately.

Security

  • All communication with our server uses HTTPS encryption
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • No cookies or tracking mechanisms are used

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal data at all, the extension is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact

This extension respects your privacy and operates with transparency. We believe in minimal data collection and maximum user control.
